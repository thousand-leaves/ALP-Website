Alice Landen is a freelance designer and illustrator based in Brighton, UK. Working as a textiles designer for the last ten years, she has created prints for many international brands and retailers. Currently living a nomadic lifestyle, she loves wandering around tropical gardens and mediterranean patios, exploring the beauty of plants and everyday objects. Alice draws much of her inspiration from botanical elements, creating detailed drawings in soft color palettes. Each print is hand-drawn before being digitally colored.
